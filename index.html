About

Who am I

Nico Spezzacatena is a multi-talented percussionist, performer, and educator from Hoboken, New Jersey. He received his BFA and MFA in world music performance from the California Institute of the Arts. Nico has been studying and researching traditional music and dance for over 25 years.

What I do

I am available to give workshops for schools, after school programs, and corporate events. Workshops in West African drum and dance (Ewe, Dagomba, and Manding), Afro-Cuban drumming, North and South Indian rhythmic principles, and Kecak (Balinese Monkey Chant) are currently offered. These workshops are fun and educational and essential for students of all ages and backgrounds.
